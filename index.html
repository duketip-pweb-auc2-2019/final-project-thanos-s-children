About Us

We are a good school and would really enjoy teaching your kids. We started in 1800 B.C. teaching dinosaurs Thanos used the time stone to go back in time and start this school from scratch. He has made it the best school ever and he has made many tweaks to it but keeping the staff. He made it to his own vision. Our Principal Thanos has helped the world with this elementary school. He is the best of any one of us. Our school is a boarding school and each grade is a different infinity stone. We go up to seventh grade. This means kindergarten is power stone, 1st grade is time stone, 2nd grade is mind stone, 3rd grade is space stone, 4th grade is reality stone, 5th grade is soul stone, 6th grade is the infinity gauntlet, and 7th grade is the Child of Thanos. Once you join our school you choose a clan and during the whole year you are doing clan challenges. Once you complete this you can move to our high school.*COMING SOON*. In 7th grade you learn about Thanos' history and his life choices and how he completed his mission or destiny. This is the last year of the school of the universe. You can then go to our different private highschools that spend on different subjects.
