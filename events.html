EVENTS

  • In Jotober we start school. This is the orientation for the newcomers to join clans. Once you join a clan you stay in that clan.
  • In Mapril everyone knows their rival clan and the clan wars begin on the last day of this month
  • In Debuary Debra the school libraian gives us assignments to do for clan points.
  • Squidtober is where we eat squids and do water related activies to get clan points.
  • Juluary is where we learn about the infinity stones.
  • September is where you have a break from the challenges. You have more months but these are their starter months.
