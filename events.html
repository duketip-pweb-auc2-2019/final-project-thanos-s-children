EVENTS

  1. In Jotober we start school. This is the orientation for the newcomers to join clans. Once you join a clan you stay in that clan.
  2. In Mapril everyone knows their rival clan and the clan wars begin on the last day of this month
  3. In Debuary Debra the school libraian gives us assignments to do for clan points.
  4. Squidtober is where we eat squids and do water related activies to get clan points.
  5. Juluary is where we learn about the infinity stones.
  6. September is where you have a break from the challenges. You have more months but that will be added later
